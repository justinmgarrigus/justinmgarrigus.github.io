Introduction

Hello! My name is . I'm a PhD student at the University of Texas at Austin currently studying computer architecture. My advisor is Dr. Lizy Kurian John, and my work is with the Laboratory of Computer Architecture. During Spring/Summer 2026, I'm working with AMD as a research associate intern developing scale-up/scale-out GPU systems.

I'm interested in developing exciting things in computer architecture, which makes my research difficult to specify in concrete terms. I want my dissertation to consist of vary different kinds of projects without any kind of throughline, because there are too many interesting things happening in this field to narrow down on any single topic. However, my current projects are focused on adding new instructions to hardware systems, which involves connecting computer microarchitecture, processor/memory systems, software optimization, compilers, and more together.

My Master's thesis is (1) an exploration of deep-learning compilers, (2) an L2-cache microarchitecture for reducing duplicated values in lowered im2col convolution, and (3) an analysis of distributed GPU systems evaluating LLM workloads, and was advised by Dr. Hui Zhao at the University of North Texas. My Bachelor's thesis is a detailed investigation of programming languages, syntax vs. semantics, and the introduction of a custom programming language maximizing expressibility of any programming problem, advised by Dr. Barrett Bryant.
